🎯 Start New Session
Each student plays 5 questions × 4 categories = 20 total. Categories rotate from your chosen starting category. After all 4 categories, the final scoreboard shows the Top 3 students overall.
🎮 Controls
End Session: stop the current student mid-quiz and return to the idle screen.
Reset Leaderboard: wipe all stored Top 3 scores. This cannot be undone.
🎛 Hardware Reference
4 Buttons (MCQ questions): A, B, C, D — student presses the button matching their chosen answer.
2 RFID Cards (True/False questions): Card A and Card B. The display tells the student which card means what for each question.
Tip: Use 2 differently-coloured blank cards (e.g. blue + red) so students can tell them apart at a glance.
